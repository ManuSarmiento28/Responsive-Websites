About the Artist

Noah Kahan is an American singer-songwriter known for his folk-pop and indie sound, mixing acoustic instruments with emotional lyrics. He started by sharing his music online, which helped him build an audience before signing with a record label. A lot of his songs focus on mental health, growing up, relationships, and feeling stuck in a small town, so many people relate to his music because it feels honest and personal.

Over time, his music started to go viral on social media, especially on TikTok, and he gained even more fans through touring and live performances. Songs like “Stick Season” and “Call Your Mom” helped make him more well-known, and his music is often described as comforting but also a little sad in a relatable way.

Why I Like This Artist

I like Noah Kahan’s music because it feels very real and emotional. His songs are easy to listen to but also make you think about your own life. I usually listen to his music when I’m stressed or just want something calm in the background.
